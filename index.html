Welcome to my personal website!

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Princeton Program of Plasma Physics. I am currently working for Prof. Matthew W. Kunz in the Department of Astrophysical Sciences, Princeton University.

My main research interest is the high-performance simulation of fundamental plasma processes, mainly in astrophysical systems.

I grew up in Malang, Indonesia before obtaining my Bachelor of Arts (A.B.) for Physics from Princeton University in 2018. Outside of my research, I have various interests which can be seen from my activities and personal blog posts.

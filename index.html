Welcome to my personal website!

I am a sixth-year graduate student in the Princeton Program of Plasma Physics currently working for Prof. Matthew W. Kunz in the Department of Astrophysical Sciences, Princeton University.

My main research interest is in the high-performance simulation of fundamental plasma process, mainly in astrophysical systems.

I grew up in Malang, Indonesia before obtaining my Bachelor of Arts (A.B.) for Physics from Princeton University in 2018. Outside of my research, I have various interests which can be seen from my activities and personal blog posts.
